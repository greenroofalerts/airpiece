How it works

Speak. See. Know.

Mounted on your hard hat or chest strap. Always watching, always listening, always ready. You talk — it acts.

01

You speak

Say anything naturally. Log an observation, ask a question, request a safety check. Always listening in active mode.

02

It sees

A wide-angle camera captures what's in front of you. Every voice note is paired with a geotagged image of exactly what you're looking at.

03

AI thinks

Vision AI analyses the scene. Identifies species, spots hazards, reads text, understands context. Cross-references with your project history.

04

You hear

Response comes back through a bone conduction earpiece in seconds. Keep working. Keep your hands on the job.

Talk to it

Just say it.

Pure voice. Speak like you'd speak to a colleague standing right next to you.
